Creating a Paper Plane!

Quickly make a high-flying paper plane!


You will need...

  • A Single Sheet of Paper









Step 1

Lay out your single sheet of paper. It can be colored, but it doesn't have to be.



Step 2

Fold the top two corners toward the middle of the paper so that they meet, and crease the paper.



Step 3

Now, fold the paper down the middle, so that the crease and edges of the corners are towards you.



Step 4

The next step is to fold the sides of the paper in half, up towards the middle crease. This makes the wings of the paper airplane.



Step 5

Repeat the last step on both sides of the airplane, so that it is symetrical.



Step 6

Now fly that plane!!
